About Me

Ever since I was a child, I have been passionate about software development. The idea of creating something that can solve real-world problems through code has always fascinated me.

Alongside my love for development, I also discovered a natural talent for hairdressing at a young age. I realized this talent when I had the chance to plait someone's hair, a moment that was like a dream come true. My journey in hairdressing has been just as creative and rewarding as my journey in tech.

Over time, as I learned and watched others style hair, my passion for software development only grew stronger. I have come to understand that my creativity knows no bounds, whether I'm designing a beautiful hairstyle or crafting an elegant user interface.

Now, I am on a path to becoming a UX/UI developer, where I can merge my technical skills with my artistic vision. I believe that my unique combination of passions—software development and creativity in hairdressing—will help me deliver intuitive and aesthetically pleasing user experiences.

Agri-Mentor

The Problem:

Farmers in Kenya face numerous challenges, including unpredictable weather, pest infestations, and volatile market conditions. They often lack access to real-time, reliable advice tailored to their specific needs and local climate conditions. Existing solutions are fragmented, making it difficult for farmers to obtain cohesive and actionable information.

Our Solution:

Agri-Mentor is an AI-driven decision support system that provides Kenyan farmers with personalized, real-time agricultural advice. By utilizing Go APIs and blockchain technology, the platform offers tailored recommendations on crop management, pest control, and market trends, ensuring data security and accessibility.
